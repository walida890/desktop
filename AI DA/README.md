# Meridian Financial — AI Data Analyst Capstone

**5 Tasks · 10 Weeks · Real DA Portfolio**

---

## Programme Overview

A capstone DA programme where you work as a Junior Data Analyst at Meridian Financial, a UK fintech operating under FCA regulation. You will build a professional analytics portfolio across the full data project lifecycle, using AI to accelerate research, analysis, and documentation at every step.

AI is embedded as your working environment throughout — not an afterthought.

---

## The Scenario

**Meridian Financial** — a UK fintech providing personal loans, mortgages, and insurance. The FCA has issued a 90-day regulatory notice requiring Meridian to demonstrate compliance with Consumer Duty principles across its data handling, reporting, and customer communications.

You are brought in as a Junior DA to support the regulatory response — analysing existing data, identifying gaps, and producing the evidence pack the compliance team needs to submit.

## Getting Started

1. All files live inside the `AI DA/` folder
2. Open the `AI DA/notebooks/` folder — each task has its own `.ipynb` notebook
3. Work through each notebook sequentially
4. Complete all TODO cells and export outputs before moving on

---

## AI How to Use

Every notebook contains:
- AI prompt cells - copy these into ChatGPT, Claude, or Copilot to accelerate research and drafting
- Critique checklists - always review AI output before submitting; AI can hallucinate regulatory citations and generate plausible-but-wrong analysis
- Red lines - documented cases where AI consistently fails on BA/DA tasks

**Critical:** You must be able to defend every number and every claim. AI-generated content is a starting point, not a submission.
