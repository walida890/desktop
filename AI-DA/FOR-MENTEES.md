# How This Programme Works — Simple Explanation

---

## What Are All These Files?

Think of this programme like a **school project that lasts 10 weeks**. You are playing the role of a Junior Data Analyst at a fake company called Meridian Financial.

---

## The Main Folder: AI-DA/

Everything is inside the AI-DA folder. Here is what each thing does:

---

### The README (you are reading this)
This file. It explains everything in plain English.

---

### 00-PROGRAMME-OVERVIEW.md
The big picture. Who the company is, who your boss is, what the problem is (FCA regulatory deadline).

---

### 01-TASK-1-DISCOVERY.md  through  05-TASK-5-PRESENTATION.md
These are your **homework assignments** for weeks 1 through 10.

Each one tells you:
- What your boss asks you to do that week
- What you need to produce (your deliverable)
- Example AI prompts you can use to help you

---

### AI-PRACTICE-GUIDE.md
Tips on how to use AI (like ChatGPT) properly. The main rule: **AI is a helper, not a replacement**. You still need to check everything it gives you.

---

### notebooks/ folder
This contains **Jupyter notebooks** (.ipynb files). These are like interactive documents where you can:
- Read instructions
- Fill in your own answers
- Run code to make charts and diagrams
- Export your work as CSV files

---

## How Do I Actually Do the Programme?

### Step 1 — Open a notebook
Go into the `notebooks/` folder and open `Task_1_Discovery_ASIS_Analysis.ipynb` using:
- **Jupyter Notebook** (installed on your computer)
- **VS Code** (free, has a notebook viewer)
- **Google Colab** (free, runs in your browser — no install needed)

### Step 2 — Read the cells
Each notebook is split into cells. Some cells have instructions, some have code, some are empty for you to fill in.

### Step 3 — Do the work
When you see a cell that says **TODO**, that is your job to complete. You might:
- Write some text
- Fill in a table
- Run a code cell
- Export something to CSV

### Step 4 — Use AI to help
When you see an **AI Prompt cell**, copy that prompt and paste it into ChatGPT or Claude. It will give you a starting point. Then you review it, change it, and make it better.

### Step 5 — Export and submit
At the end of each task, export your notebook outputs and send them to your mentor.

---

## What Are CSV Files?

CSV files are simple spreadsheets saved as plain text. When you run the export cells in the notebooks, they save CSV files to your computer. These become the inputs for the next task.

---

## Why Is AI In Every Task?

Because in real data analyst jobs today, you use AI every day. This programme teaches you how to use it properly — not just blindly trusting it, but checking it and improving it.

A portfolio of 5 professional DA deliverables:

You can show these to an employer as proof you can do the job.
